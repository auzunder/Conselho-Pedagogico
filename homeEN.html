We will accept donations!

List B, a competitor to the pedagogical council, decided to accept donations from any and all students who are part of ESEC and are willing to help in the implementation of the measures proposed by us.

After being elected to the list for the school board, we will publish a new news about and how you can donate.

Donations can be made via Paypal, bank transfer, or cryptocurrencies. The list wants to show that with everyone's help it can go further in its measures and financially help the school with related projects.

We will be open to accepting Bitcoin, Ethereum and Dogecoin. All donation accounts will have public transactions and balance, creating transparency between the list and those who help financially.

EASIER ACCESS TO NECESSARY EQUIPMENT

ESEC FOR ALL
Student motivation is essential for a good performance, therefore, it is intended to provide a pleasant space where interaction and integration between students from different courses, and also with teachers, is possible and beneficial. The space could be reserved for possible events and gatherings, such financial income would be destined to the AE and to projects belonging to the institution. We aspire to give more emphasis and act on the forms filled in by students about the ESEC and its functioning.

BETTER GUIDANCE FOR FRESHMAN STUDENTS
We intend to have specific days before the start of the school year so that guided tours can be made to introduce new students to the new space and how it works.
RESTRUCTURING OF UNIVERSTITY COURSES
×
RESTRUCTURING OF UNIVERSTITY COURSES
We propose that there is the option of having at least 1 semester, in which the student can focus on his/her area of interest for part of the course, with the student having the ability to choose the units that he/she most identifies with, within the selected area. . This measure would allow the student to feel more competent within their area of interest. We will also allow the student to make their own schedule, following a limit of options, so that they can reconcile their academic life with their personal life.

CREDIT AND DEBIT CARD PAYMENTS AT UNIVERSITY SERVIÇES

BETTER SCHEDULE, BETTER LIFE
We will make sure that we can allow the student to have greater control over his schedule, that is, that he has the ability to make his own schedule, following a limit of options, so that he can reconcile his academic life with his personal life. Such a measure would give the student greater motivation, as well as make life more accessible.

NEW BUILDING FOR ESEC
The existing conditions in ESEC building II of our institution present a negative note regarding the quality of our infrastructures. We will call for a rehabilitation of the building or for students to have classes in a much more pleasant and comforting space, in order to improve their performance.

BETTER INTEGRATION FOR ERASMUS STUDENTS
We propose the existence of tutors or some type of monitoring for students coming from the ERASMUS program, as well as calling for the integration of students in the classes. Such a measure would increase the prestige of ESEC outside the country.
